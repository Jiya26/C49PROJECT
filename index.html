Cicada

Features Of Cicada

=>Cicadas are medium to large in size, ranging from 2 to 5 cm.

=>Cicada, any of a group of sound-producing insects that have two pairs of membranous wings, prominent compound eyes, and three simple eyes.

=>Male cicadas produce loud noises by vibrating membranes near the base of the abdomen.

=>Eggs are usually laid in woody plant tissues that drop from the plant when, or shortly after, the eggs hatch.
